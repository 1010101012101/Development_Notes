Do you want to spend incredible time in Batticaloa as a volunteer? Join our team and our organisation!

Volunteering and internship opportunities in Sri Lanka at our new college.
We are cooperating with many community organizations, schools, social enterprises to help them to provide free/low-cost education services for communities with the participation of international volunteers and experts.
We welcome kind-hearted volunteers from around the world who are caring and willing to give and receive important life lessons.

We provide you with accommodation and free meals (3 times a day). We expect the volunteers to stay with us at least one month as teaching and socializing with students takes time - we would appreciate it. Sharing your professional skills and teaching talents, the volunteers with or without formal teaching experience can contribute greatly to increase our students' possibilities to seize many wonderful opportunities.

Volunteers will be required to teach English to students ages 6 and above. These students come from various backgrounds and abilities. Teaching experience is not essential but volunteers should be fluent in English.
Ideally volunteers will stay for at least ONE month, longer stays are appreciated as it allows students to build rapport with their teacher.

Volunteers are expected to help 4-5 hours a day, five days a week. Usually this is between 2-7pm, so volunteers are free in the mornings and early afternoons to go to the beach or relax. On days off we can recommend places to go, and potentially help with accommodation and/or transport.

Since this is an English school, and we have a strong emphasis on correct oral communication and pronounciation, we require a short informal Skype chat to see if we are both compatible, and if you have any questions these can be answered too.

Aside from the teaching side...

Batticaloa is one of the bigger towns on the East Coast of Sri Lanka. There is a beach a 15 min cycle from the place you will be staying (we will provide bicycles). There are also markets, temples, churches and more around Batticaloa.

We can also help provide activities such as fishing, rowing a traditional Sri Lankan fishing boat, explore different food and culture.

Aside from Batticaloa, there are other nearby towns:

Passikudah beach is about a 45 min drive (our past volunteers have hitchhiked easily, or there is a bus). This is a beautiful well-known beach with great snorkelling.

Arugam Bay is a world-wide known surfing spot, and is 3 of hours south, so a weekend trip is definitely possible.

Kandy is the next biggest city after Colombo and has many cultural and tourist experiences, such as temples, food, festivals and more.

Overall, Sri Lanka is a fairly small island and many more places can be reached within a few hours so many options with your time off. Have a chat to us and we can work out whats best for you, and potentially help with transport and accommodation in various places.
