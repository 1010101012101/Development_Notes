We are one of the most eco friendly hotels in Sri Lanka home to travellers who search for the road less travelled, those who want authenticity in their holiday, those who seek new adventures taking only pictures and leaving only lasting footprints in our hearts.

We are located close to Yala National Park, nestled inside a man made forest in the middle of the dry zone. Our mud chalets are designed in line with true Sri Lankan rural homes which means an “open air” concept. Our tree houses are guest favourites and never fail to wow the kids.

We were listed as one of the top 10 most extraordinary treetop hotels in the world by Trip Advisor in both 2013 and 2014 and we are nominated for the "World Luxury Hotels Award for 2016"
