Welcome to Villa Eco Green Negombo. It is a lovely and small guesthouse which is located very close to the Negombo beach. Just 05 minutes ride away from the famous hotels restaurants and shops. I also have bicycles and I am always willing to provide them to you for sight seeing.
I will provide you 3 meals a day and tea coffee and other refreshments. Also a comfortable double room will be provided with a fan. I can accept 2 volunteers at a time and the priority will be given to the couples.
What you have to do here is really simple. Just help me with daily cleanings gardening watering plants kind of stuff. If you can deal with guests it will be also a plus point. Creative minds are always welcome here. Always love to learn something from others. You only have to help me 05 days a week 04 05 hours per a day. I am very flexible and friendly guy. You will know it soon after you arrive here. Looking forward to meet you. If you are the right guy or couple please do not hesitate to send me a message for more details. Thank you.
