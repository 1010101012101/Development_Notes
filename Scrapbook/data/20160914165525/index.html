Hi,

I am looking for people to help me running our tourist resort and organic farm in the beautiful southern hill country in Deniyaya.
It's a place for different workshops to be run, we have a bakery included in the resort where many local people shop and are just about to start a small organic farm in the backyard.
We are looking forward to meeting interesting people and to learning a variety of things including such as organic farming, sustainable building, forest conservation, buttefly farming, water conservation, bakery, eco-resort, advertising the place through the internet ect.
We are looking for experienced people, who are willing to take ownership of the tasks to fulfill and having a lot of freedom to organise themselves accordingly.

Furthermore, we are starting a campaign to save the Rainforest (see information below) through awareness-raising as a first step. Therefore we are especially looking for people who would like to take part in that, e.g. go to schools and talk to the kids about the importance of the topic, create games to get them involved...

Apart from that, there will be plenty of time to explore the surroundings by bike or by foot. There are plenty waterfalls nearby to swim in, nature, rice paddies and tea plantations to hike through and of course the Rainforest National Park - a paradise for nature lovers!


We also have a guesthouse/ hostel at the beachside near Matara in the South and are currently looking for people to volunteer.
